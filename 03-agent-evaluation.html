Why evaluate an agent?

An agent that "feels right" in demos can fail badly in production. Evaluation turns vibes into measurable scores you can track over time.

"It works on my demo" isn't enough

In demos, agents look magical. In production, they hit weird edge cases — wrong facts, slow responses, surprising bills. Evaluation makes the invisible visible.

Four pillars: quality (DeepEval, RAGAS), completion (did the agent finish?), latency (is it fast?), and cost (is it affordable?). All four matter together.

Key Insight

  • Demos hide failures; evaluation finds them
  • Quality, speed, and cost are all trackable
  • Tracking over time = a regression alarm

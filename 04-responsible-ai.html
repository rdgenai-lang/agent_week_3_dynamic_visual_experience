From "agent does anything" to "agent does the right things"

Power without responsibility is a lawsuit waiting to happen. A well-designed agent comes with four guardrails that catch the most common failure modes before they reach users or production systems.

PII masking hides sensitive data. Injection defence filters hostile prompts. Audit logs record every decision. Human-in-the-loop pauses for risky actions. Together, they turn a clever toy into a trustworthy product.
